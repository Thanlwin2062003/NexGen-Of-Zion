About Our Ministry

Called to serve in Christ's name since 2005

Our Mission

We exist to bring the light of Christ to remote and underserved communities through free English education, youth fellowship, and compassionate outreach.

Inspired by Matthew 5:14, we aim to empower the next generation spiritually and practically so they can shine as lights in their communities and live out God's purpose for their lives.

"You are the light of the world. A city set on a hill cannot be hidden."

- Matthew 5:14 (ESV)

Our Approach

We serve through three core ministry areas:

  • English for the Margins

    We offer free English classes to children and youth in remote areas, helping them gain skills that open opportunities for education, work, and communication beyond their villages.

  • Youth Fellowship & Discipleship

    We host regular youth gatherings focused on spiritual growth, leadership, and building Christ-centered friendships, encouraging young people to grow strong in faith and character.

  • Community Outreach

    We walk alongside families and communities by offering practical support, encouragement, prayer, and presence. We believe true ministry means showing up with both the gospel and compassion.

Our approach is rooted in relationship, built on faith, and driven by a desire to see lives transformed by God's love.
